Scrum

Scrumis a framework for developing, delivering, and sustaining products in a complex environment, with an initial emphasis on software development, although it has been used in other fields including research, sales, marketing and advanced technologies.
It is designed for teams of ten or fewer members, who break their work into goals that can be completed within time-boxed iterations, called Sprints read more, no longer than one month and most commonly two weeks.
